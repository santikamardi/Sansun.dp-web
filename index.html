About SMK Grafika Desa Putera

SMK Grafika Desa Putera is a renowned vocational high school that focuses on preparing students for careers in the graphic design and multimedia industries. Our mission is to provide high-quality education and hands-on training to ensure our graduates are job-ready and globally competitive.

About Us

Misi:
